Clean Green Alliance (2022-2023)

During my time in 12th Grade, I was a member of a group that specializes in coastal clean ups which I consider aligned with Sustainable Development Goal (SDG) 13: Climate Action. Cleaning up shores involves gathering necessary equipment like gloves, trash bags, and recyclables containers, and dividing the area into sections for efficient coverage. We volunteers collect litter, including plastics, glass, and fishing debris, while being mindful of hazardous waste. Documenting the clean-up, sharing before-and-after photos, and educating the public about waste reduction are key steps. coastal clean-up also contributes to SDG 13 by promoting climate resilience through the protection of coastal environments. It was conducted in Aplaya, Bauan, Batangas

SDG 13: Climate Action

SDG 13, "Climate Action," calls for urgent action to combat climate change and its impacts. Rising temperatures and extreme weather events threaten ecosystems, economies, and vulnerable communities, worsening poverty and inequality. The goal emphasizes the need for global cooperation to reduce emissions, promote renewable energy, and build resilience to climate impacts. Achieving SDG 13 is essential for ensuring a sustainable future and protecting the planet for future generations.
